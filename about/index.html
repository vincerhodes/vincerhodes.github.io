About / Join

Right Court SC is a recreational squash club for intermediate-to-advanced players who want structured, coached sessions rather than just open court time. We run themed sessions that focus on one part of the game at a time — length, volleys, drops, boasts, movement, front-court play, deception, and serves & returns — so every week has a clear focus, not just open hitting.

Who it's for

Players who already have solid racket skills and want to sharpen a specific part of their game under coaching, rather than complete beginners. If you can rally consistently and know the rules, you're in the right place.

When & where

Wednesdays 18:00–20:00 & Saturdays 09:00–11:00 — Feeling Squash. Session plans are posted in advance on the Drills & Sessions page.

Dress code

We lean classy: crisp all-white is the house preference, in the old-school squash tradition. That said, we're not sticklers — wear what you like, with one firm exception: no exceptionally short shorts. For the avoidance of doubt, “exceptionally short” means shorts so brief they leave nothing to the imagination and risk ‘popping out’ mid-rally. Everything else is fair game.
